Business Use Case

The platform identifies companies with increasing trading volume by comparing current turnover against historical averages.

Multi-period rolling averages enable detection of abnormal growth patterns and early momentum signals.

Designed for investors seeking structured, data-driven market analysis instead of manual spreadsheet tracking.
